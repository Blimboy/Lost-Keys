The Lost Key QR Code

The Lost Key QR code can be engraved into a metal tag to be attached to your set of keys, when it's scanned on a mobile phone it will open a web page containing;

- Your phone number

The finder can then call you directly or they can click a button on the page to send you a text message with their current GPS location details.

How to use the QR Code?

It's simple, you just download the QR code image and use it to create your tag. The only details you'll be asked to enter are;

Your Mobile Number.

Your details will only ever appear on the screen of the person who scans your tag. They're not captured by us, or shared in any way.

You can order a tag, either by using the link provided on the QR code page, or send it to any local engraving service to make a tag of your choice.
